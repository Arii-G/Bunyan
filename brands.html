Hamza AS is a locally manufactured, globally certified WaterWise solutions for its sound proof, hot water piping system that is suitable for all pressurized sewer pipes available in dimensions DN 50 to DN 250. The system offers a reliable backbone for water delivery in efficient and innovative means, providing easy installation, reduced weight compared to cast iron and secure connections with reliable push-fit jointing to optimize drainage performance.
